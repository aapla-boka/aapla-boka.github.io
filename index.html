"While hundreds of students are busy writing their board exams and practical submissions, apsara the boka has more important things to attend to 💤. I went to him to give him food, he opened his eyes briefly and closed them. 'Your food is not worth disturbing my resting state' he said in his wise brain."

— Science Student, Batch of 2027

"Boka was happily eating cat food, when suddenly a dog came to steal his food! He got scared and climbed onto his tree. But not for long, as he mustered up courage to jump down and fight the dog. He hissed and wiggled his back with aggression. Sadly that did not deter the dog and both of them started eating the food they were fighting for. The dog left after finishing the food, and boka returned to his sleeping spot. Thus, displaying a rare moment where the king shared his food with the peasants."

— Science Student, Batch of 2027

"It was a quiet afternoon, and as I walked toward the gate, I saw Boka standing all alone in front of a parked rickshaw. There was nobody else around, and he was just standing there perfectly still, looking very serious. Where was he planning to go to? Visit his friends in Egypt?"

— Science Student, Batch of 2027

"I love cats and dogs, my friend and I come for a walk daily. It is a cute cat, always on the branch of tree, it's safe place. Nowadays I bring for it a treat!"

— Regular Visitor & Friend of Boka

"His highness boka has a special earthen pot to keep his water at optimum temperature. He can be seen drinking from it after a hearty meal, which happens unsurprisingly often."

— Science Student, Batch of 2027
